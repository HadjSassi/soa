Our Program

  • Day 1 :
    1. Take a dip on a beach far away from the hotels. See how the migratory birds gather. Gossip with the fishermen to learn their traditional ways…
    2. Go on a pirate boat trip to take a swim on a beautiful wild beach.
    3. Explore sandy trails by bike. Admire the small countryside mosques.
    4. Watch the jewellers of Houmt-Souk or the potters of Guellala at work.
    5. Attend the feeding of crocodiles at the Djerba Explore centre! This leisure park is home to a Nile crocodile farm.
    6. Visit a ‘houch’, the traditional house of Djerba. These all-white houses, consisting of vaulted rooms around a patio, are perfectly suited to the hot climate.
